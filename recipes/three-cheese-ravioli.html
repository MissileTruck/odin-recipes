Three Cheese Ravioli

Make pasta from scratch! Roll, fill, and cut traditional Italian ravioli.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/3 cup whole milk ricotta
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in small bowl. Throw away shells and wash your hands.
  2. Measure 1 1/4 cup flour with accuracy. Spoon into measuring cups, use a flat edge to level. Do not overpack.
  3. Add flour, salt, oil, and eggs to large bowl. Stir with spoon until a shaggy dough forms. Gather into a ball.
  4. On a lightly floured surface, knead dough until smooth and elastic, 8-10 min.
  5. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, make filling. Add ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan to small bowl. Stir and combine.
  7. Divide dough into 6 pieces. On a lightly floured surface roll 2 pieces into very thin rectangles, slightly larger than the mold.
  8. Set one piece of dough on top of mold, covering the zigzags. Use fingers to lightly press dough into holes.
  9. Add 1 level teaspoon cheese filling to each hole
  10. Set second piece of dough on top. Roll with rolling pin, pressing in all directions until zigzags are visible.
  11. Remove top of mold. Flip upside down and pop out ravioli. Repeat steps 7-11 twice to make 24 ravioli.
  12. Bring pot of slated water to boil. Cook ravioli 2-3 min, until floating. Drain in colander and serve with warm marinara. TASTE & SHARE!
